# menstrual health, well-being and companion application
## Outline
This mobile health application has been developed to help females track and manage their menstrual cycles, generate monthly reports detecting inconsistency in the user’s logged in data, and have an AI companion to have safe and confidential conversations with. The application has several features such as a calendar logbook, a mood and hydration logbook, AI chat bot, weekly activities, and health centers.
## ATTRIBUTES
- *Calendar book*: Log monthly dates and data regarding user’s cycle.
- *Mood and hydration logbook*: Logs the user’s mood based on their preference (by the hours, daily, weekly, bi-weekly or monthly), suggest hydration using push notification every 2 hours to achieve the 2.7 liters recommended daily (This amount increases when the user is on their cycle as it is recommended to have an additional 1-2 liters daily during the menstrual cycle. This concludes to a push notification every 1.5hours).
- *AI chat bot*: Friendly and welcoming agent that answer questions and stores the data of users, uses the data from applications backend to offer personalized feedback during conversations and reminds users of appointments during conversations or as push notifications.
- *weekly activities*: The user gets customized diet and exercise to follow to help cope with the mood level, stress level, bloating, headaches and cramps (during and off cycle).
- *Health centers*: Uses the user’s location to pinpoint GP, hospitals and accidents and emergency’s locations around their vicinity.
